## 📝 Customization Guide

### 1. **Business Name**
Find and replace:
- `Bouncy Fun Hire` → Your business name
- `info@bouncyfunhire.com.au` → Your email
- `0400 XXX XXX` → Your phone number

### 2. **Pricing**
Update prices in the HTML:
- Search for `$250`, `$280`, `$320`, etc.
- Change to your actual prices

### 3. **Service Area**
Find:
- `Sydney & Surrounding Suburbs` → Your area

### 4. **ABN**
Find:
- `ABN: 12 345 678 901` → Your real ABN

### 5. **Testimonials**
Replace the example testimonials with real ones:
```html
<p class="testimonial-text">"Your real review here..."</p>
<p class="testimonial-author">- Real Name, Location</p>
```

### 6. **Images (Optional)**
Currently using emojis for castles. To add real photos:
- Replace emoji divs with `<img>` tags
- Add images to a `images/` folder
