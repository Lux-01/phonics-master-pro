# App Ideas from Pain Points Research

*Generated: 2026-03-24*
*Source: Pain points identified from current trading workflows*

## 1. Trade Setup Automator ⭐ HIGH PRIORITY

**Pain Point:** Manual wallet setup, DEX navigation, slippage config takes 5-10 minutes per trade

**Solution:** One-click position entry with preset configs (size, stop loss, take profit)

**Features:**
- Pre-configured trade templates
- One-click execution
- Auto slippage calculation
- Batch position entry

**Target Market:** DeFi traders, bot operators

**Monetization:** Freemium ($9/month), execution fees

**Status:** 💡 Idea

---

## 2. Rug Pull Radar

**Pain Point:** Scanner catches rugs too late; manual red flag checking is tedious

**Solution:** Real-time token audit + auto-take-profit on rug signals

**Features:**
- Multi-source rug detection
- Automatic position exit on signals
- Early warning alerts
- Community-reported flags

**Target Market:** Meme coin traders

**Monetization:** $29/month subscription

**Status:** 💡 Idea

---

## 3. Portfolio Commander

**Pain Point:** Tracking scattered positions across multiple wallets; no unified P&L view

**Solution:** Multi-wallet dashboard with P&L, tax reporting, performance analytics

**Features:**
- Unified multi-wallet view
- Real-time P&L tracking
- Tax report generation
- Performance analytics and charts

**Target Market:** Anyone with 2+ wallets

**Monetization:** $19/month; tax season spike pricing

**Status:** 💡 Idea

---

## 4. Alpha Hunter Feed

**Pain Point:** Scanner results buried in JSON files; no mobile alerts

**Solution:** Mobile push notifications for Grade A+ signals with 1-tap ape

**Features:**
- Mobile-first design
- Instant push notifications
- One-tap trade execution
- Signal history and stats

**Target Market:** Mobile-first traders

**Monetization:** $49/month for instant alerts

**Status:** 💡 Idea

---

## 5. Clone Wallet Copier

**Pain Point:** Manually tracking whale wallets; no automation for copy trading

**Solution:** Clone any wallet's trades automatically with size scaling

**Features:**
- Wallet tracking and monitoring
- Automatic trade replication
- Size scaling (e.g., 1% of whale size)
- Performance analytics of cloned wallets

**Target Market:** Copy traders

**Monetization:** 0.5% per executed trade

**Status:** 💡 Idea
