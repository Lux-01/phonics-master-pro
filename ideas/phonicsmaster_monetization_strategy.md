## 🚀 Launch Strategy

### Phase 1: Soft Launch (Month 1-2)
- **Price**: $4.99/mo (no annual yet)
- **Goal**: Validate product-market fit
- **Promo**: 50% off first month ($2.50)
- **Target**: 500 downloads, 5% conversion

### Phase 2: Growth (Month 3-6)
- Add annual option ($39.99)
- Introduce Family Plan ($9.99)
- Launch "Founding Member" campaign: $19.99 lifetime
- **Target**: 10,000 downloads, 8% conversion

### Phase 3: Scale (Month 7-12)
- Add in-app purchases (lesson packs, avatars)
- School/B2B outreach begins
- Affiliate partnerships (educational toys)
- **Target**: 50,000 downloads, 8% conversion
