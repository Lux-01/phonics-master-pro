## 🛵 Uber Eats Integration

The website includes **multiple Uber Eats links:**

1. **Top Banner** - "Get your kebabs delivered with Uber Eats!"
2. **Delivery Section** - Dedicated Uber Eats card
3. **Direct Link** - `https://www.ubereats.com`

### To Connect YOUR Uber Eats:

Replace this link:
```html
<a href="https://www.ubereats.com" target="_blank">
```

With your actual Uber Eats restaurant link:
```html
<a href="https://www.ubereats.com/au/store/your-restaurant-name" target="_blank">
```

**How to get your link:**
1. Log into Uber Eats Manager
2. Go to "Marketing" → "Share"
3. Copy your restaurant link
4. Replace in the HTML

## 📝 Customization Guide

### 1. **Business Name**
Find and replace:
- `Sydney Kebab House` → Your business name
- `info@sydneykebabhouse.com.au` → Your email
- `0400 XXX XXX` → Your phone number
- `123 Main Street, Sydney NSW 2000` → Your address

### 2. **Menu Prices**
Update prices in the HTML:
- Search for `$14.50`, `$13.50`, etc.
- Change to your actual prices

### 3. **Menu Items**
Add/remove menu items as needed:
- Find the menu sections
- Copy/paste menu-item blocks
- Update names, prices, descriptions

### 4. **Opening Hours**
Find the hours-list section and update:
```html
<li>
    <span class="day">Monday - Thursday</span>
    <span class="time">10:00 AM - 10:00 PM</span>
</li>
```

### 5. **Social Media**
Update social links in footer:
```html
<a href="your-facebook-url">📘</a>
<a href="your-instagram-url">📸</a>
```
