Our Story

Founded in 2015, Sydney Kebab House brings authentic Turkish and Lebanese flavors to the heart of Sydney. Our recipes have been passed down through generations, using traditional spices and cooking methods.

Every kebab is made to order with premium Halal-certified meats, fresh vegetables, and our signature garlic sauce. We charcoal grill our meats for that authentic smoky flavor you can't replicate.

Whether you're after a quick lunch, late-night feast, or catering for an event, we've got you covered. Come taste the difference! 🥙
