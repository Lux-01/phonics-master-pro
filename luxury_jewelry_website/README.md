## 📝 Customization Guide

### 1. **Business Name**
Find and replace:
- `Aurum & Co.` → Your business name
- `aurumco.com.au` → Your domain
- `concierge@aurumco.com.au` → Your email

### 2. **Contact Information**
Update in HTML:
```html
<p>+61 2 9000 0000</p>           <!-- Phone -->
<p>123 Luxury Lane, Sydney</p>   <!-- Address -->
<p>Mon–Sat: 10:00 AM – 6:00 PM</p>  <!-- Hours -->
```

### 3. **Product Prices**
Update prices:
```html
<p class="product-price">$12,500</p>
```

### 4. **Product Names**
Change product names:
```html
<h3 class="product-name">Eternal Diamond Solitaire</h3>
```

### 5. **Collection Images**
Replace emojis with actual images:
```html
<div class="collection-image">
    <img src="your-image.jpg" alt="Diamond Collection">
</div>
```

### 6. **Social Media**
Add your social links in footer:
```html
<a href="your-facebook-url">📘</a>
<a href="your-instagram-url">📸</a>
```
