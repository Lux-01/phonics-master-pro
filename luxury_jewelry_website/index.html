Our Heritage

A Legacy of Excellence

Since 1985, Aurum & Co. has been Sydney's destination for exceptional jewelry and timepieces. Our master craftsmen combine traditional techniques with contemporary design to create pieces that transcend generations.

Every diamond is GIA certified, every gold piece is ethically sourced, and every watch is authenticated by our Swiss-trained horologists. We don't just sell jewelry—we curate heirlooms.

Visit our boutique in the heart of Sydney for a personalized consultation, or explore our collections online. Your journey to timeless elegance begins here.

— The Aurum Family
