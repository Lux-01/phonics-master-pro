## 🚨 RUG PULL LESSONS - ALIENS & XTuber

**What Happened:**
- 04:27: Scanner found 2 Grade A tokens (ALIENS, XTuber)
- 05:08: Both tokens rugged (confirmed by Tem)

**Scanner Analysis:**
| Token | Age | Top 10% | Grade | Red Flags |
|-------|-----|---------|-------|-----------|
| ALIENS | 0.2h (11 min) | 27.4% | A | 🚨 TOO NEW |
| XTuber | 1.0h | 72.9% | A | ⚠️ TOO NEW, Whale concentration |

**Root Causes:**
1. **Age filter too permissive** - 11-minute-old tokens shouldn't get Grade A
2. **Top 10% threshold too high** - 72.9% whale concentration is a massive red flag
3. **Grade inflation** - "TOO NEW" warning didn't prevent A-grade

**Lessons Learned:**
- Tokens < 30 minutes old = automatic reject (not just warning)
- Top 10% > 50% = automatic reject (whale concentration = rug risk)
- Need "survivor checkpoint" - tokens must exist 1h+ before grading
- Volume/MCAP ratio can be faked in first hour
