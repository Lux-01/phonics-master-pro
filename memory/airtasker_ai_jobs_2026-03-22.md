## Executive Summary

**Key Finding:** Writing and Business services have the clearest AI-executable paths, with established pricing and demand.

## COMPETITIVE ADVANTAGES FOR AI:

### 1. Speed
- **Human average:** 2-3 days for a business plan
- **AI delivery:** Same day or within 6 hours

### 2. Research Capability
- Can synthesize information from 10+ sources instantly
- No fatigue on large documentation projects

### 3. Consistency
- Brand voice remains identical across all deliverables
- No quality variance day-to-day

### 4. Revision Capacity
- Can handle 5+ revision cycles without complaint
- Instant turnaround on changes

### 5. Volume
- Can work on 3-5 tasks simultaneously
- No scheduling conflicts

---

## CHALLENGES FOR AI ON AIRTASKER:

1. **No Profile/Presence** - Need human front-end to post and manage
2. **Airtasker Insurance** - Platform requires human tasker identity
3. **Revision Cycles** - Need mechanism for client feedback
4. **Payment Handling** - Need human-linked bank account

### Workaround:
Template responses, pre-written proposals, standardized processes where human reviews before sending.

---

## RECOMMENDATION:

**Best Entry Categories:**
1. **Resume/CV Writing** (Easiest, fastest, repeat demand)
2. **Content Writing** (Blog posts, website copy - straightforward)
3. **Business Plans** (Higher ticket, more research-intensive)
4. **Grant Writing** (Specialized, high value)

**Avoid:** Physical tasks, in-person meetings, licensed work (legal/medical)

**Sweet Spot:** Research-intensive writing tasks where AI's information synthesis is superior to human speed.
