### Key Strategy Rules (v2.0)

**Entry Requirements (ALL must be met):**
1. Quality: $20M+ market cap (WIF, POPCAT, BONK, BOME, SLERF, PENGU, MEW)
2. Trend: Price above 1h EMA20
3. Volume: 2x average volume confirmation
4. Signal: Dip -10% to -18% OR green after 2 reds
5. Sector: Max 1 position per category

**Position Sizing:**
- A+ Setup: 0.5 SOL
- B Setup: 0.25 SOL
- C Setup: Skip
- Max 3 positions

**Exit Strategy:**
- Scale 1: Sell 50% at +8% profit
- After Scale 1: Move stop to breakeven
- Scale 2: Trailing stop, tighten at +15%
- Hard Stop: -7% (before scale), breakeven (after)
- Time Stop: 30 minutes max hold

**Risk Management:**
- No entries first/last 30 min of hour
- Reduce size 50% if win rate <40% (10-trade lookback)
- Pause 10 min after 3 consecutive losses
- Daily loss limit: -0.3 SOL
- Max drawdown target: <15%
