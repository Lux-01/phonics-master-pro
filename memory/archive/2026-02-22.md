### 🎯 DIP Agent Analysis (Needs Upgrade)

**Performance:**
- Win Rate: 6% (3W/47L)
- Total P&L: -2.1244 SOL (-212%)
- Profit Factor: 2.18 (wins ARE bigger than losses)

**The Problem:**
- Hard stop at -7% triggers on **94% of trades** before mean reversion
- Winners hit +20% scale-out, then trail (3 trades worked perfectly)
- Paradox: Profit factor > 2 means strategy has potential, but win rate unsustainable

**Exit Breakdown:**
- Hard Stop (-7%): 47 trades (all losses)
- Trail Stop: 3 trades (all wins)

**Upgrade Path:**
1. **Widen hard stop** from -7% to **-10%** (use ATR-based)
2. **Lower scale target** from +20% to **+10-15%**
3. **Add entry confirmation** - wait for 1-2 bullish candles after dip

**Verdict:** Strategy has potential (profit factor > 2) but parameters too tight. Need Strategy v1.1 with wider stops.
