## Issues Discovered

1. **Auto-sell not in original script**
   - v1 only bought, didn't sell
   - Added monitor functionality

2. **Token address simulation**
   - DexScreener token-profiles endpoint returns mock addresses
   - Need pairs endpoint for real addresses

3. **API data quality**
   - During off-peak hours (3-4 AM Sydney), APIs return limited/no real data
   - Market conditions: Low volume, no quality setups meeting criteria

4. **Price volatility**
   - Low-cap newly launched tokens dropping fast
   - Most hit SL (-5% to -18%) immediately after entry
