Our Story

From seed to cup, a journey of passion

It Started with a Single Bean

Founded in 2018 by coffee enthusiast Marcus Chen, Bean & Bloom began as a small roasting operation in a garage in Portland, Oregon. What started as a hobby quickly grew into an obsession with bringing out the best flavors in every bean.

Today, we've grown into a team of 12 passionate coffee professionals, but our mission remains the same: to source the finest beans ethically, roast them to perfection, and deliver fresh coffee that makes your morning extraordinary.

Our Values

Sustainability

We're committed to carbon-neutral roasting and 100% compostable packaging by 2025.

Fair Trade

We pay 25% above market rate to ensure farmers and their communities thrive.

Quality First

We cup every batch before it leaves our roastery. If it's not exceptional, it doesn't ship.

Community

We donate 1% of sales to local food banks and coffee education programs.
