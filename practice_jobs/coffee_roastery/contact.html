Frequently Asked Questions

How fresh is your coffee?

We roast every Tuesday and Thursday. Your coffee is shipped within 24 hours of roasting and typically arrives 2-3 days after that. We include a "roasted on" date on every bag so you know exactly how fresh it is.

Do you offer wholesale?

Yes! We supply coffee to cafes, restaurants, and offices throughout the Pacific Northwest. Contact us wholesale pricing and to set up a tasting session.

What's your shipping policy?

We offer free shipping on all orders over $50. Orders under $50 have a flat rate of $5.99. We ship via USPS Priority Mail, which typically takes 2-3 business days.

Can I modify my subscription?

Absolutely! You can change your coffee selection, frequency, or pause your subscription at any time through your account dashboard or by contacting us.
