About Restored Right Sydney

Your single point of contact for professional restoration services Sydney-wide

Our Story

Restored Right Sydney was founded on a simple principle: when disaster strikes, you need someone you can trust. As an owner-operated business, I personally oversee every project, ensuring the highest standards of workmanship and customer care.

With over 3,500 insurance claims assessed and years of hands-on experience, we understand both the technical aspects of restoration and the stress our clients face. That's why we offer a single point of contact - you deal directly with me from the first call to the final walkthrough.

Our IICRC certification means we follow industry best practices, and our direct relationships with insurance companies help streamline what can otherwise be a complicated claims process.
