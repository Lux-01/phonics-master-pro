What To Do First

Follow these steps while you wait for our arrival

Ensure Safety

Turn off electricity if safe to do so. Avoid standing water near electrical outlets. If in doubt, evacuate and wait for professionals.

Stop The Source

If possible, turn off the main water supply. For burst pipes, locate your water meter and shut off the valve immediately.

Document Everything

Take photos and videos of the damage for insurance purposes. Don't throw anything away until documented.
