# Business Strategy Engine (BSE)

**"Strategic thinking for your operation."**

Analyzes what's working and suggests strategic improvements for income, efficiency, and growth.

## Capabilities

- **Revenue analysis:** Track income streams and ROI
- **Market insights:** Identify opportunities
- **Growth planning:** Strategic roadmap creation
- **Competitive analysis:** Benchmark against alternatives
- **Pivot suggestions:** When to change direction

## Output Format

```
Quarterly Strategy Review
├── Current Performance
├── Market Analysis
├── Recommendations
├── Risk Assessment
└── Action Plan
```

## Usage

```bash
python3 bse.py --quarterly-review

python3 bse.py --analyze-market

python3 bse.py --suggest-pivots
```
