# ACA Research Plan: 15 Autonomous Money Loops

## Objective
Research and synthesize 15 autonomous income loops with detailed analysis of revenue potential, difficulty, setup requirements, and strategic recommendations.

## Research Questions
1. What are the revenue ranges for each loop?
2. What are the true barriers to entry? (technical, capital, time)
3. Which loops are realistically achievable with OpenClaw capabilities?
4. What are the risk-adjusted ROI calculations?
5. Which loops stack together synergistically?

## Methodology
- Analyze each loop independently
- Cross-reference with user's existing capabilities
- Compare difficulty vs reward
- Identify quick wins vs long-term plays
- Create implementation priority matrix

## Expected Output
1. Detailed analysis of all 15 loops
2. Feasibility assessment for OpenClaw
3. Priority ranking based on user's current skills
4. Quick-start implementation guide for top 3 loops

## Data Sources
- User-provided loop descriptions
- OpenClaw capability inventory
- OpenClaw skill ecosystem
- Market research on revenue models
