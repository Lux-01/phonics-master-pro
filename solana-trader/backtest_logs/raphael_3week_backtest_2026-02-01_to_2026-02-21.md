## New Rules Discovered (Rules #28-35)

### High-Impact Rules (Immediate Implementation)

**Rule #28: Weekend Grade C Ban**
- Don't trade Grade C setups on weekends (Sat-Sun)
- Weekend volume is insufficient for reliable execution
- Saved from 3 losses in backtest

**Rule #29: RSI Requirement for Mean Reversion**
- Mean reversion entries require RSI(14) < 45
- Prevents buying "falling knives" in strong downtrends
- Improved Grade A win rate by 12%

**Rule #30: No Overnight Grade C**
- Grade C positions must exit before 22:00 UTC same day
- New launches are unpredictable overnight
- Prevents gap-down surprises

**Rule #31: Emotional Circuit Breaker**
- After 2 consecutive losses: mandatory 30-minute break
- No exceptions, no "just one more"
- Prevents revenge trading spirals

**Rule #32: Evening Trading Curfew**
- No new positions after 20:00 UTC
- Exception: A+ setups only
- Late-day liquidity is thinner, slippage higher

### Medium-Impact Rules

**Rule #33: Chop Day Protocol**
- Identify chop days early (3+ doji candles on 1H)
- Reduce position sizes 50% on chop days
- Tighten stops to 5% (from 8%)

**Rule #34: A+ Extended Targets**
- A+ setups can use 15% profit target (vs standard 10%)
- Use trailing stop after 10% profit
- Let winners run when conviction is highest

**Rule #35: Creator Wallet Deep Check**
- Verify creator wallet funding source
- Reject if funded from known rug/scam addresses
- Cross-reference with rugcheck.xyz database

**Rule #36: Holiday Volatility Boost**
- During major market holidays, A+ targets can extend to 20%
- Volatility is opportunity when risk-managed
- Requires full attention/monitoring

## Mistakes Documented (To Avoid)

### Mistake #1: Grade C Overtrading
- **Frequency:** 29 trades (22.8% of total)
- **Result:** Net loss of 0.044 SOL
- **Impact:** Reduced overall return by ~2.3%
- **Solution:** Raise minimum grade to B, only take C on perfect confluence

### Mistake #2: FOMO Entries
- **Example:** Trade #052 (BONK), entered before full signal confirmation
- **Cost:** -5.2% when waiting would have avoided trade
- **Pattern:** 4 occurrences in 21 days
- **Solution:** Use limit orders, never market buy on FOMO

### Mistake #3: Ignoring Narrative Timing
- **Example:** Counter-trend trades during strong AI/DePIN rotations
- **Cost:** -3.1% average on 6 trades
- **Solution:** Check Crypto Twitter sentiment, only trade with narrative

### Mistake #4: Friday Afternoon Risk-On
- **Pattern:** Lower win rate on Friday 14:00+ UTC
- **Cause:** Weekend risk-off, lower liquidity
- **Solution:** No Grade C after Thursday, reduce sizes Friday

### Mistake #5: Overstaying After Time Stop
- **Example:** Trade #024 held 18 minutes past 4-hour limit
- **Result:** Turned +2.1% into -1.2%
- **Solution:** Automated alerts at 3:45, force exit at 4:00

### Mistake #6: Not Tracking Correlation
- **Issue:** Multiple meme coin positions simultaneously
- **Result:** Correlated drawdowns when meme sector corrected
- **Solution:** Max 2 positions per sector, track sector correlation
